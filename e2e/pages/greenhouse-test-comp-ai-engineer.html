About Test-Comp

Test-Comp was founded in 2022 and has rapidly established itself in cybersecurity, securing $85M in funding and rallying a dedicated team of 100 professionals. Our mission is to create safer digital environments for small and medium businesses globally through our Secure & Insure platform.

The Role

We are looking for an AI Engineer to help us build advanced AI solutions using Large Language Models and agentic workflows. You will work on backend infrastructure for generative AI initiatives, bringing strong foundations in machine learning, deep learning, and data analysis.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and deploy cutting-edge AI solutions into production environments
  • Build, fine-tune, and assess end-to-end AI/ML pipelines for scalable systems
  • Construct agentic systems using modern frameworks like LangGraph and PydanticAI
  • Implement multi-step and multi-agent systems with robust orchestration
  • Research emerging AI advancements and incorporate them into product development
  • Collaborate across R&D teams to integrate AI capabilities with product features

Requirements

  • 5+ years of professional experience as an ML Engineer, Data Scientist, or Software Engineer with a focus on production deployment
  • 1-2 years of hands-on experience with LLMs (GPT, Llama, Gemini, etc.)
  • Proven experience building production-grade LLM applications (RAG systems, AI agents, copilots)
  • Strong Python proficiency at production level
  • Solid background in software engineering, data science, or ML engineering
  • Familiarity with Agent AI architecture, including MCP standards
  • Adaptability, initiative, ownership mentality, and ability to thrive in dynamic environments

Nice to Have

  • Cybersecurity domain expertise
  • Experience with LLM frameworks and AI orchestration platforms (LangChain, PydanticAI, LangGraph)
  • Observability and monitoring design for LLM agents
